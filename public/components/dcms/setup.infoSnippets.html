Setup info snippets

Info snippets is a DCMS Foundation Entity which comes with a set of predefined DCMS info snippets types. You can choose whether or not you want to use the predefined set of snippet types. You can also create customized snippet types of your own.

Info snippets created in DCMS are always associated to the organization and person, if identified. Additionally, snippets can be associated with other objects related to the activity during which they were created, such as a payment, a dispute, or a promise to pay.
