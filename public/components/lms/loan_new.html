When creating a loan with a term type balloon payment, enter a balloon term that is longer than or equal to the loan term. When creating a loan with an amount type balloon payment, enter a balloon amount greater than the requested loan amount.

Subtype indicates whether the loan is secured by collateral. If you select Secured, enter the loan-to-value ratio required for the loan in Collateral Percentage.
